Indxx Thematic Industry Classification System (ITICS) is Indxx’s proprietary industry classification taxonomy that offers precise categorisation of the global equity universe, based on their operating segment(s) and revenue stream(s). There are two levels of standard industry classification in this taxonomy: L1, a broader level theme, and L2, a more granular level sub-theme. These have been defined after an in-depth analysis of macro-economic as well as industry specific trends.


Problems associated with current industry classification frameworks:

Broad Classification Systems

Single-sector classification of a company does not provide a holistic picture of the company’s overall business lines, making peer comparison difficult.
Following old school and traditional classification systems deprive investors of current thematic trends.

Manual Research

Thematic research is a complex process, especially when conducted for over a thousand companies.

ITICS BENEFITS

Empower your business with useful insights and right data.





MULTIPLE THEME CLASSIFICATION

The ITICS taxonomy classifies companies into a broad range of themes and sub-themes. The mapping of each company to themes/sub-themes in the ITICS taxonomy is based on extensive research conducted by our experienced team.

RISK EVALUATION

A standard industry classification helps investors get a holistic picture of any company’s business exposure. Thus, helping them gauge their risk exposure as per ongoing macro-economic trends.

MARKET ANALYSIS

It is also useful in conducting sector analysis and staying up to date with current market trends.

BETTER DECISION MAKING

A granular classification helps gain a deeper insight into a company’s business exposure, thereby enhancing decision making.

Data Compilation

The ITICS taxonomy levels are identified by analysing macro-economic/industry trends and revenue generating sources of the global equity universe. Each company’s reported operating segment and its revenue is compiled via publicly available data and mapped to a unique level. The revenue attributed to each ITICS level for a company is calculated by adding the revenues of all operating segments which map to that level.
